All I need to know about life and problem-solving, I learned from Mario

  1. Things that initially seem impossible are often just a matter of practice.
  2. Sometimes you will be dropped off with no directions and your job is to figure it out by trying things.
  3. Don’t panic – not only will you look ridiculous, it will be counterproductive.
  4. Go off the beaten path and look around often.
  5. Sometimes, all you need is the right strategy.
  6. Sometimes, all you need is patience.
  7. But look for shortcuts.
  8. Read the documentation and learn your action moves.
  9. You don’t need to get every last coin – know when you’ve done enough, keep your eyes on the prize, and get to that next boss fight.
  10. When all else fails, Google.
  11. Time box it.
  12. Don’t forget to have fun!
